## Content To Pull Through

- HealthLink is one available referral path and should be easy to copy.
- HealthLink EDI must remain prominent: `inshealh`.
- Online referral form, intake email, phone and fax should be presented clearly so referrers can choose what works for them.
- The page should feel calm, premium, clinical, restrained, and useful to busy referrers.
- The main promise is structured intake, thoughtful clinician matching, clear communication, and multidisciplinary care.
- Add a "what happens next" pathway: received, intake review, matching, contact/booking.
- Add a "what to include in referral" checklist before launch.
- Keep public wording conservative around timing, availability, clinician status, and funding eligibility.
- Preserve safe campaign attribution from landing page to form through hidden non-clinical fields.

## Do Not Pull Into Public Page Without Review

- Detailed clinician availability unless verified.
- Funding/accreditation badges unless verified.
- Time-based claims such as same-day triage or 1-2 week availability unless operationally true.
- Internal HubSpot sync details beyond a simple privacy/data-boundary statement.
- Patient/client clinical examples.
- Practice Brain, Tacklit, Halaxy, Zanda, custom platform, or CEO command-centre detail.
