# Decisions

## Current Direction

- HealthLink is the preferred secure referral pathway.
- The online form is a secondary route when HealthLink is unavailable.
- Phone and email support are the fallback for unclear, complex, or attachment-heavy referrals.
- HubSpot should be treated as referrer/campaign CRM only unless a separate data policy approves otherwise.
- Clinical referral details should not be placed into marketing systems by default.
